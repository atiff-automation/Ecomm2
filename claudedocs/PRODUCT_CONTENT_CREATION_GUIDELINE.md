## Overview

### Purpose
This guideline ensures consistent, SEO-optimized, and factually accurate product content across all JRM HOLISTIK products for maximum search visibility and customer conversion.

### Core Principles
‚úÖ **Factual Only** - No hallucination, only real product information
‚úÖ **SEO Optimized** - Keywords integrated naturally in Bahasa Malaysia
‚úÖ **Non-Repetitive** - Each product has unique positioning
‚úÖ **User-Focused** - Clear benefits, easy to scan, mobile-friendly
‚úÖ **Concise** - 550-650 words target (quality over quantity)

## Meta Title Guidelines

### Requirements

| Criteria | Specification |
|----------|---------------|
| **Length** | 60-70 characters (MAXIMUM 70) |
| **Language** | Bahasa Malaysia primary, English brand name acceptable |
| **Format** | `[Product Name] JRM | [Key Benefit] [Category/Use Case]` |
| **Keywords** | Include 1-2 main keywords naturally |

### Formula

```
[Product Name] JRM | [Primary Benefit] untuk [Target User/Condition]
```

### Examples ‚úÖ

**Good:**
- `Cenanga Oil JRM | Minyak Sejuk untuk Sakit Kepala, Mual & Bayi Kembung` (70 chars)
- `Gel Bidara Arab JRM | Selepas Mandi Wajib, Ruqyah & Rawat Kulit` (69 chars)
- `Silky Sunscreen JRM SPF15 | Pelindung Matahari Semula Jadi Malaysia` (70 chars)

**Bad:**
- `Cenanga Oil` (too short, no benefit)
- `Cenanga Oil JRM is the best aromatherapy cooling oil for headache migraine nausea` (too long, English)
- `Minyak Sejuk Cenanga Oil JRM` (product name not first)

### Best Practices

1. **Product name first** - Always start with product name for brand recognition
2. **Include "JRM"** - Brand consistency across all products
3. **Main benefit second** - What problem does it solve?
4. **Target/use case third** - Who is it for or when to use
5. **Use separators** - Pipe symbol `|` for readability
6. **Check character count** - Use online counter, max 70 characters
7. **Avoid ALL CAPS** - Use proper capitalization only
8. **Include numbers if relevant** - "SPF15", "5% AHA" adds specificity

## Meta Description Guidelines

### Requirements

| Criteria | Specification |
|----------|---------------|
| **Length** | 150-160 characters (MAXIMUM 160) |
| **Language** | Bahasa Malaysia |
| **Format** | Product name + key ingredients + main benefits + CTA/use case |
| **Keywords** | Include 2-4 keywords naturally |

### Formula

```
[Product Name] - [Key Ingredients]. [2-3 Main Benefits]. [Target User/Use Case].
```

**OR**

```
[Product Name] dengan [Ingredients]. [Benefits 1], [Benefit 2]. [Use Case/CTA].
```

### Examples ‚úÖ

**Good:**
```
Cenanga Oil roller - Minyak aromaterapi sejuk dengan Pudina, Camphor & Wintergreen.
Legakan sakit kepala, mual, mabuk. Selamat hamil & bayi kembung perut.
(158 characters)
```

```
Gel Bidara Arab - Untuk kesegaran SELEPAS mandi wajib/hadas. Ruqyah, perlindungan
rohani, rawat jerawat badan & eczema. Ekstrak Bidara Arab tulen.
(158 characters)
```

**Bad:**
- `Cenanga Oil is good` (too short, no value)
- `This is the best cooling aromatherapy oil from JRM HOLISTIK that helps with many problems` (no specifics)
- Very long description exceeding 160 characters that gets cut off in search results... (truncated)

### Best Practices

1. **Lead with product name** - First 2-3 words matter most
2. **Highlight unique ingredients** - What makes it special?
3. **List specific benefits** - Not generic claims
4. **Include use case** - "Untuk", "Sesuai", "Selamat untuk"
5. **End with differentiator** - What sets it apart?
6. **Use active voice** - "Legakan", "Rawat", "Kurangkan"
7. **Avoid repetition** - Don't repeat meta title exactly
8. **Check mobile preview** - How it looks on phone search

## Product Description Structure

### Target Length: 550-650 words

**Sections (in order):**

1. **Mengenai Produk** (SHORTENED - 1 paragraph, 80-100 words)
2. Khasiat dan Manfaat (5-8 bullet points)
3. Bahan Utama/Ramuan (3-7 ingredients)
4. Cara Penggunaan (Clear steps)
5. Keselamatan & Pensijilan
6. Sesuai Untuk (5-7 user profiles)
7. Kenapa Memilih [Product] JRM HOLISTIK (5 USPs)
8. Soalan Lazim (3-4 Q&A pairs)

---

### 1. Mengenai Produk (‚ö†Ô∏è IMPORTANT: KEEP SHORT!)

**Length:** 80-100 words MAXIMUM (1 paragraph only)

**Purpose:** Quick introduction that covers:
- What is this product
- Who makes it (JRM HOLISTIK / Jamu Ratu Malaya)
- Key ingredients (2-3 main ones)
- Primary benefits (2-3 top benefits)
- Target users

**Formula:**
```markdown
[Product Name] adalah [product type] dari JRM HOLISTIK (Jamu Ratu Malaya) yang
diformulasi dengan [2-3 key ingredients] untuk [primary purpose]. Diperkaya dengan
[supporting ingredients], [product] membantu [benefit 1], [benefit 2], dan [benefit 3].
Sesuai untuk [target users], ia [unique feature] - menjadikan [product] sebagai
[positioning statement].
```

**Example (90 words):**
```markdown
Cenanga Oil JRM adalah minyak aromaterapi berasaskan herba dari JRM HOLISTIK
(Jamu Ratu Malaya) yang diformulasi untuk memberikan kelegaan fizikal dan emosi
melalui sensasi sejuk dan aroma menyegarkan. Dalam bentuk roller yang mudah
digunakan, diperkaya dengan Mentha Piperita, Camphor dan Wintergreen, minyak
aromaterapi ini membantu melegakan sakit kepala, mual, mabuk kenderaan serta
selamat untuk wanita hamil dan bayi kembung perut - menjadikan Cenanga Oil
sebagai minyak aromaterapi serbaguna yang wajib ada dalam setiap keluarga.
```

**‚ùå DO NOT:**
- Write 3 paragraphs (too long!)
- Repeat same opening: "...ialah produk yang diformulasi..."
- Use generic descriptions
- Exceed 100 words

**‚úÖ DO:**
- Keep to 1 flowing paragraph
- Start with unique angle per product
- Include brand mention naturally
- End with positioning/value proposition
- Vary opening structure for each product

## Content Writing Best Practices

### Language & Tone

‚úÖ **DO:**
- Write in natural Bahasa Malaysia (conversational, not translated)
- Use active voice ("Kurangkan", not "Boleh mengurangkan")
- Be specific and factual
- Use simple, clear language
- Include local context (iklim tropika, keluarga Malaysia)

‚ùå **DON'T:**
- Use Google Translate (unnatural)
- Use overly formal or technical language
- Make exaggerated claims ("terbaik dunia", "100% guarantee")
- Copy-paste descriptions between products
- Use English unnecessarily

---

### Keyword Integration

**Natural Placement:**
1. Product name in H1 heading
2. Primary keyword in first paragraph (Mengenai Produk)
3. Secondary keywords in benefit headers
4. Long-tail keywords in FAQ questions
5. Keywords in alt text (images)

**Keyword Density:** 1-2% (natural, not forced)

**Example Keywords by Product Type:**
- Face products: "kulit wajah", "muka", "jerawat", "jeragat"
- Body products: "kulit badan", "sakit sendi", "lenguh"
- Hair products: "rambut gugur", "rambut lebat"
- Spiritual: "ruqyah", "mandi wajib", "bidara"

---

### Unique Positioning (IMPORTANT!)

Each product MUST have unique:

1. **Opening angle** (Mengenai Produk)
   - Product A: Problem-solution angle
   - Product B: Heritage angle
   - Product C: Scientific angle
   - Product D: Multi-use versatility angle

2. **Brand mention phrasing** (avoid repetition)
   - "dari JRM HOLISTIK (Jamu Ratu Malaya)"
   - "produk JRM HOLISTIK yang..."
   - "dari Jamu Ratu Malaya - JRM HOLISTIK"
   - "[Product] JRM adalah..."

3. **USP emphasis**
   - Focus on what makes THIS product different
   - Not generic benefits all products share

4. **FAQ topics**
   - Product-specific questions
   - Comparison with similar products

### 1. Keyword Research
- Use Google Keyword Planner (Bahasa Malaysia)
- Check competitor product pages
- Include local keywords (Malaysia, Malaysian)
- Long-tail keywords for specific problems

### 2. Heading Structure
```

```

### 3. Internal Linking
- Mention related products in descriptions
- Link to product categories
- Cross-sell in "Cara Penggunaan" section

Example:
```markdown
**Double Cleanse (Untuk Solekan Tebal):**
- Langkah 1: Gunakan Mellia Micellar Water untuk tanggalkan makeup
```

### 4. Mobile Optimization
- Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences max)
- Bullet points for scannability
- Clear headings
- No long blocks of text

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

### ‚ùå Meta Title/Description Mistakes

1. **Too long** - Gets cut off in search results
2. **Too short** - Wastes valuable space
3. **Keyword stuffing** - "Minyak panas JRM minyak urut minyak herba..."
4. **English** - Use Bahasa Malaysia
5. **Generic** - "Produk yang bagus untuk kesihatan"
6. **Missing CTA** - No compelling reason to click
7. **Duplicate** - Same meta across multiple products

### ‚ùå Content Mistakes

1. **Long Mengenai Produk** - 3 paragraphs is too much! Keep to 1 paragraph.
2. **Copy-paste** - Reusing same structure/phrases across products
3. **Hallucination** - Adding ingredients/benefits not provided
4. **Vague benefits** - "Membuat kulit lebih cantik" (too general)
5. **Missing specifics** - No frequency, dosage, timing
6. **Wall of text** - Long paragraphs without breaks
7. **No product differentiation** - All products sound the same
8. **Forgetting [UPDATE] tags** - Not marking missing information

### ‚ùå Language Mistakes

1. **Google Translate Bahasa** - Unnatural phrasing
2. **Mixed language** - Random English words unnecessarily
3. **Overly formal** - Like academic paper
4. **Marketing superlatives** - "Terbaik!", "100% berkesan!"
5. **Passive voice** - "Boleh digunakan" vs "Gunakan"

### Example: Product Comparison FAQ

